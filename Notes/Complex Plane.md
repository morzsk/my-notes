**Distance between two Complex Numbers**
â†“
- The vertical distance squared + the horizontal distance squared
- Equals the difference between the two complex numbers
$$
\begin{align} \\
w-z&=(a+bi)-(c+di) \\
&= (a-c)+(b-di)\\
|\omega-z|&=\sqrt{ (a-c)^2+(b-d)^{2}}
\end{align}
$$
